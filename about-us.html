THE STORY OF RHYTHM House

Rhythm House is a music store founded by 5 students with the aim of providing the best music experience possible, the store caters to all ages, you can almost find anything. Records range from CDs, poetry tapes for children, the latest pop music collections for teenagers, to rock collections of 60s, 70s can also be found here etc.

CRITERIA FOR COMMITMENT

In case you cannot find the music you need, we will order it within a week.

We not only provide music but also competitions and live shows. In addition to music, you can find a number of music-related magazines for sale. You can also register as a member to enjoy all the benefits such as earning points or pre-ordering products at any time as well as your other transactions.
